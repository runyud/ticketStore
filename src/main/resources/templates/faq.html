Shopping

Q: How do I place an order?

A: You need to register for an account and confirm your email and password. After you log in, you can order any tickets.

Q: How do I register for an account? 

A: There is a my account page on the upper right hand side, click on it and then the left tab is where you can register. Use the temporary password provided in the confirmation email and confirm your account through the attached link.

Q: How long does it take to process and ship orders?

A: It usually takes around 1 - 3 business days (Monday-Friday) for order processing. Then for ground service you can receive it around 5-7 days, for premium shipping, it will only take about 1-2 days.

Q: Are all of the tickets guaranteed in stock?

A: We try to update the website as often as possible to reflect out of stock conditions. However, the ability to order an item does not guarantee that it will be in stock when your order is processed. You will be notified if an order (seat) is not available, and we will give you a full refund.

Q: Why can't I choose the seat I want?

A: If you are unable to select a specific seat for a particular event, it usually means that the event ran out of seats.

Q: I can't find a ticket for a specific event on this website, are there tickets available that are not listed on the website?

A: The items that are currently shown on our website represent what is currently available to view and order online. There is a wider selection of merchandise available at the Ticket Master and the IMG Learfield. Please visit those sites for more events.

Q: How can I check the status of my order?

A: If you have registered for an account at our ticketStore, click on "My Account" and log in with your email and password. You can view the status of any orders that were made while you were signed in. You can also contact us at 1.800.112.8456 with your name, order number, and a request for status of your order. Our office hours are Monday - Friday 8am - 5pm PDT.

Q: How can I cancel or change my order?

A: You can cancel your order or make a change to it by contacting us at 1.999.999.9999 as soon as possible after your order is placed with your name, order number, and a request for cancellation or a description of the request. Our office hours are Monday - Friday 8am - 5pm PDT. We will do our best to cancel or modify your order and provide a full refund if possible.

Shipping 

The TicketStore is proud to offer you competitive shipping rates. All of our shipments are professionally packed to ensure the safety of your items in route.

Q: How much is the shipping charge on orders?

A: The shipping charge depends on the total amount of the merchandise ordered as well as the shipping method chosen.

Q. What is your shipping method?

A: We ship UPS, FedEx or US mail .

Q: When will I receive my order?

A: On average, your order will be received within 1-7 business days. During September and March, delivery time may take longer.
